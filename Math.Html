The Genius of Indian Mathematics

This is a visual representation of Bhāskara II's contributions to mathematics and astronomy, showcasing his profound impact on the field.

Highlights of Contributions

  • Līlāvatī: Poetic explanations of arithmetic and geometry.
  • Bījagaṇita: Advanced algebra and equations.
  • Siddhānta Shiromani: A comprehensive treatise on astronomy and mathematics.
  • Introduced early concepts of calculus and infinitesimals.
  • Provided innovative methods for solving quadratic and cubic equations.
  • Developed techniques for calculating the positions of planets and eclipses.
  • Explored permutations, combinations, and cyclic methods for equations.
  • Devised an accurate approximation of pi (π) and studied its applications.
  • Proposed a refined understanding of astronomical phenomena like planetary motion and eclipses.
  • Introduced methods for handling irrational numbers and infinite series.

Legacy

Bhāskara II's pioneering work has inspired mathematicians across generations. He is celebrated for his innovative ideas and the beauty of his mathematical expressions. His legacy continues to be a cornerstone in the fields of mathematics and astronomy, influencing modern research and studies.
