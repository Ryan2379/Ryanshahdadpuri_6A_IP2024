India's Contribution to Satellite Technology

Introduction

India has made significant contributions to satellite technology with key projects like GSAT and NAVIC. These innovations help in communication, navigation, weather forecasting, and disaster management.

NAVIC - India's Regional GPS System

NAVIC provides accurate position data for India and its surrounding regions. It enhances navigation and supports various services:

  • Improved transportation and logistics
  • Mapping and surveying
  • Disaster management and tracking

Impact on Weather Prediction & Disaster Management

Satellites like GSAT and NAVIC help in:

  • Weather Prediction: Collecting data for accurate weather forecasts to aid agriculture and aviation.
  • Disaster Management: Monitoring cyclones, floods, and earthquakes, facilitating efficient disaster response.
  • Rescue Operations: Providing real-time location data to assist rescue teams during emergencies.
